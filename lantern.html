About the Lantern Festival

Chinese Lantern Festival is celebrated 2 weeks after Chinese New Year, as it marks the end of Spring Festival.

The main celebration of the Lantern Festival focuses on lighting lantern. Every year, streets are decorated with colorful and unique designs of lanterns, often with riddles written on them. It won't be truly a Chinese Festival without watching dragon and lion dances, set off fireworks, and not eating a special dish called tangyuan, which is a sweet rice balls.
