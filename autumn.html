About Mid-Autumn Festival

The Mid Autumn Festival, commonly known in China as the Moon Festival, is also one of the most important celebrations in Chinese Culture. Just like the Spring Festival, families gather together to moon gaze and enjoy mooncakes.

Legend of Chang'e

There's a lot of famous legend stories in China among them belongs to a Goddess, every Mid-Autumn festival, tale of the Moon Goddess are told to the younger generation. Such story contains love and sacrifice, teaching the younger generation to be grateful.

If you're interested in reading the full story of the Moon Goddess, Chang'e. You can visit: China Highlights - Mid Autumn Festival

Traditions

The people celebrate by lighting lanterns, moon-gazing, worshipping the moon and offering sacrifices, folk performances and cultural shows.
