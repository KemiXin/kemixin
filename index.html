Explore Chinese Festivals

Come and explore the beauty and traditions of Chinese culture. Learn about the festivals origin and how festivals represent China culture alot.

Culture has been with us since the beginning of human civilization. However, we do not all share the same culture, as some cultures may follow their religious beliefs.

Chinese people value their culture greatly. Every year, countless festivals are celebrated. Among in those celebrated festivals, the act of 'paying respect to their ancestors, the appearance of 'Dragons and Lions' their strong likes towards the color red, and bright colors are seen a lot in their Festivals.
